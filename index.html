      About the Guide and Region

      Local knowledge, mountain experience, and Pankisi hospitality

      Your guide

      The tours are led by a local resident of Pankisi Valley with many years of horseback riding and mountain travel experience.

      The guide studied Geography at Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University, is certified in tourism, and has worked with travelers since 2016.

      Pankisi Valley

      Pankisi is one of Georgia's most distinctive regions, known for Kist culture, mountain landscapes, rivers, villages, and generous hospitality.

      The valley brings together tradition and wild nature, making every ride feel close to both the land and the people who live there.
